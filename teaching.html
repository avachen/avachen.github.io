Teaching Philosophy

I build on students' existing experiences to help them develop an aptitude for problem solving and scientific inquiry. The essence of robotics is creative, analytical, and fundamentally grounded in real-world observations... Beyond robotics, a toolbox for life. Where and when do assumptions fall apart, what variables to modify first when approaching a new context or task. How to distill a complex problem into testable building blocks. I Scaffolding Intuition learners are familiar with self-directed materials, such as DIY videos, online classes, and textbooks. Use of search engines and generative AI. But these (as of yet) cannot directly provide informed feedback, only information. The learner has to train their own sense of discernment for whether they are on the right path.   • Teaching Development Program Advanced Track
    CTL TDP website

    The Teaching Development Program is a multiyear teaching certification associated with Columbia University's Center for Teaching and Learning (CTL). It focuses on cultivating, documenting, and reflecting upon evidence-based, student-centered learning. The TDP Advanced Track offers a teaching certification for attainment of objectives intrinsic to effective learning and pursuit of inquiry-based development as a teacher.
